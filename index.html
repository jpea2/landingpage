FAQ

Common questions, straight answers.

What if the AI voice agent says something wrong?
It's trained specifically on your business, services, and qualifying criteria. We test extensively before launch and continuously refine it based on real calls. You'll hear it before it ever talks to a customer.
Will it sound robotic?
Modern voice AI is natural and conversational. We'll let you hear it before it goes live — most people can't tell the difference.
Does the agent make outbound calls?
No. Our voice agents handle inbound calls only — specifically the ones your team can't get to. It only picks up when nobody else does.
What tools can you integrate?
Most modern business tools — CRMs, calendars, project management platforms, databases, forms, spreadsheets, and more. If your team uses it, we can likely connect it.
How long does setup take?
Voice agents: 1-2 weeks. Automation systems: 1-4 weeks depending on complexity. We'll give you a clear timeline after the discovery call.
Do I need a long-term contract?
No. Setup is a one-time fee. Support is month-to-month. No lock-ins, no exit fees.
